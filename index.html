Overview

What Happened

On the morning of February 27, a client emailed asking about an unfamiliar endorsement on his PURE renewal notice. Within a single terminal session — without opening a browser, switching between apps, or touching a mouse — the following was completed:

  1. The email was identified, read, and analyzed
  2. A full client profile was assembled from three data sources
  3. 11.6 years of email history was queried to establish the complete backstory
  4. A formal endorsement removal request was sent to the carrier in our standard format
  5. A personalized reply was sent to the client
  6. An Airtable task was created and linked to the account
  7. All system documentation was updated to reflect what happened and what’s pending
Total elapsed time from email discovery to fully documented resolution: ~30 minutes.
The advisory work — deciding what to do and how to communicate it — was still the advisor’s. The system handled the research, drafting, sending, filing, and documentation.

Workflow

What Changed

Step Before Now
Read the email Open Shortwave, read, context-switch to start research Email surfaced and read in the terminal
Understand the client Pull up Airtable, scan multiple tables, check notes Full profile assembled automatically — 12 structured sections from Airtable + Supabase + Gmail
Research history Search Shortwave for old threads, read through results, reconstruct timeline manually Client brief queries 11.6 years of email in seconds — 42 threads, 205 messages, dated and categorized
Decide what to do Advisor judgment Advisor judgment (unchanged)
Send the emails Compose in Shortwave, format manually, send separately Drafts presented for review, edited conversationally, sent from terminal
Create the task Open Airtable, fill in fields manually Task created with structured fields, linked to account
Document it Ideally update notes; realistically, move on 4 Supabase rows auto-updated — status, milestones, email log, tasks
The advisory decisions didn’t change. The time spent on research, data entry, formatting, sending, and documentation collapsed.
Implications

What This Means Going Forward

↻
Context compounds. Every client interaction writes back into the system. This account went from zero context rows to 15 in one session. Next time it comes up — renewal, claim, new request — the full story is already built. No ramp-up time.
🔍
The email archive is the long-term memory. We have indexed access to every client relationship going back to the firm’s founding. Trust history, property transactions, carrier conversations, claims timelines — all queryable by client name in seconds.
⌘
The terminal is the control surface. This entire cycle ran in a single Claude Code session. No browser tabs, no app-switching, no copy-paste between systems. The advisor stays in one place, thinks about the client, and the system handles data movement.
①
This was the first one. A straightforward endorsement removal — not a complex claim or multi-carrier renewal. But it exercised every layer of the stack end-to-end. The same workflow applies to any inbound client request.
